Controls

Movement: W,A,S,D | Rotation: Q,E or Mouse
Left Click: Place Block | Right Click: Remove Block

Instructions

  • Use WASD keys to move around the world
  • Use Q and E keys or hold left mouse button to look around
  • Left click to place a block
  • Right click to remove a block
  • Navigate carefully - you can't go through walls!
  • Toggle lighting on/off with the lighting button
  • Toggle normal visualization for debugging
  • Toggle spotlight to see directional lighting effects
